## 5. Position Sizing Commodities

```text
Risk per trade:        0.5-0.75% del capital (similar a Capa 3 Equities)
Stop ATR multiplier:   3.0-4.0 (vol alta)
Target:                2-3R

Vol anualizada típica:
  Gold:      14-18%
  Brent/WTI: 30-50% (alto)
  Copper:    20-28%
  Silver:    25-40% (muy alto)

Vol target asignado:   8-10% portfolio commodities
Max simultáneos:       3 commodities
Notional total commodities: ≤ 25% capital
```
